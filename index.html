Telecommunications Law

Experienced Telecom Lawyer in Florida with Nationwide Experience

If you own or manage a multi-dwelling unit (MDU), community association, or development, telecom decisions are part of your daily operations. From broadband access to rooftop antenna leases, these agreements impact property value, resident satisfaction, and long-term revenue.

With more than a decade of hands-on telecom experience, Derek A. Carrillo brings industry insight and deal experience to every transaction. Premier Law has guided more than $1 billion in business and real estate transactions nationwide, including broadband infrastructure projects, rooftop leases, and multifamily marketing agreements.

Key Services

Negotiate cable and broadband agreements for MDU properties
Structure access and marketing agreements
Draft bulk billing and service agreements
Negotiate wireless infrastructure and rooftop antenna leases
EV charging infrastructure agreements for MDU owners and developers

MDU & Broadband

Comprehensive representation for multi-dwelling unit telecom transactions, from initial negotiation through execution of cable, broadband, and bulk service agreements.

Wireless Infrastructure

Strategic counsel for rooftop antenna leases, small cell deployments, and wireless infrastructure transactions with major carriers and towercos.

EV Charging Infrastructure

Assisting MDU owners, developers, and associations with the legal framework for EV charging station deployment and service agreements.
