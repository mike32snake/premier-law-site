Our History

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Premier Law has spent the last decade helping clients across Florida navigate complex legal and transactional matters with clarity and confidence.

The firm was built on a simple principle: every client deserves direct access to experienced legal counsel. At Premier Law, clients receive senior-level attention and clear communication — our legal assistant is your primary point of contact, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks.

Over ten years, this approach has earned the firm a reputation for being practical, strategic, and deal-focused. With more than $1 billion in closed transactions, Premier Law has become a trusted partner for property owners, developers, telecom companies, and families throughout Florida and across the country.

Founder & CEO

Derek A. Carrillo

Derek A. Carrillo founded Premier Law in 2015 after working as a telecommunications industry attorney, where he gained firsthand experience negotiating broadband, wireless, and infrastructure deals. That real-world industry knowledge sets him apart from general-practice attorneys.

Today, Derek represents clients in three focused practice areas — telecommunications, real estate, and estate planning — bringing the same deal-focused precision to every engagement. His 11+ years of combined legal and business experience translate to practical advice, efficient transactions, and outcomes that protect client interests.

Derek founded Premier Law to deliver the senior-level attention of a large firm with the accessibility of a boutique practice. Clients benefit from direct access to experienced counsel, with our legal assistant serving as the primary point of contact to ensure responsive communication and seamless handling of every matter.
